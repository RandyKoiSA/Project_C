Export Settings from Blender to UE4
When exporting to UE4 from blender. Rename the armature to something that is not "Armature". 
In the export settings set the following:
* "Geometry->Smooth" = Face or edge. 
* "Geometry-> Tagent Space" = (Checked)
* "Armature -> turn off "deform bones" and "add leaf bones"
* "Animation -> disable "NLA strips and all actions"
